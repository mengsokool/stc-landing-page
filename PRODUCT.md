# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students, teachers, college staff, prospective students, and corporate partners. They access this system to quickly navigate to their respective services on both desktop and mobile devices.

## Product Purpose
A configurable centralized educational service portal (Quick Menu) for Thai vocational institutions. It exists to provide fast, convenient access to various systems. A critical objective is to prominently highlight the "Admission" (สมัครเรียน) section to attract and guide prospective students without hardcoding a specific institution name.

## Brand Personality
Modern educational institution, colorful, and official. It blends the vibrant, forward-looking energy of a vocational college with the credibility and structured feel of a government institution.

## Anti-references
- Old, cluttered government websites with tiny text and disorganized layouts.
- Overly futuristic, complex interfaces that hinder usability.
- "AI Slop" (generic, soulless templates, obvious AI clichés, or repetitive card grids lacking deliberate design choices).

## Design Principles
- **Clear Navigation Over Density:** Prioritize getting the user to their destination rapidly rather than packing the screen with information.
- **Strategic Highlighting:** Ensure high-priority actions—especially "Admission"—stand out unmistakably from routine administrative tasks.
- **Modern Official:** Convey both innovation and trustworthiness through a clean, vibrant, and highly structured interface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
General modern UI standards. High color contrast for clear readability and appropriately sized touch targets to ensure it is easy to click and use for everyone.
